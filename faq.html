FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is RollerCoin?

RollerCoin is an online crypto mining simulator game. The core idea of the game is distribution of block reward between players depending on their mining power - just like with real crypto mining.

How can I start playing?

Create an account at https://rollercoin.com/sign-up, customize your character and you are ready to start.

What is Mining power?

Mining power is the amount of Th/s you have. You can increase it by playing games or purchasing stationery miners that will provide a stable increase to your mining power.

What is a Block reward?

Block reward is a number of crypto coins that are distributed between ALL active miners approximately every 5 minutes. Players receive their part of block reward in accordance with the miner power they have achieved.

What is Satoshi?

Satoshi is the smallest unit of Bitcoin and it is equal to one hundred millionth of a Bitcoin (0.00000001 BTC).

How long does it take to mine the block?

The default block mining time is 5 minutes. However, just like with real Bitcoin mining, the time will decrease or increase depending on the pool power. After a while, it is be reset to 5 minutes again.

What is Multi-mining?

RollerCoin allows you to mine 3 crypto coins at the same time - BTC, DOGE, and ETH. Based on how you split your power you can select to mine one of them, two or even all three at the same time!

How can I withdraw my Satoshi?

Visit your in-game wallet page, provide your bitcoin wallet address and send a withdrawal request. The minimum amount for withdrawal is 10000 Satoshi.

Can I withdraw DOGE and ETH?

YES, YOU CAN! Go to your in-game wallet, switch to DOGE\ETH, provide your wallet address and request a withdrawal, it should appear in 3-5 business days.

Where is my withdrawal?

withdrawals are checked and sent manually. Usually, it takes up to 2 business days, but in some cases, the deadline can be extended. Please, rely on the status of your withdrawal request in your in-game wallet.
