GOAT CASH
GOAT CLASH Profile Contact About
8 bit retro greatness, that's what.
More specifically, 2 goats battle it out in a joke off to see who will remain victorious.
You, as the herder, bet on which goat you think will win.
You place your bets with $GOAT Cash and can play using Chrome, Firefox or Brave using Metamask wallet extension.
You just need to ensure you have GOAT tokens in your wallet and ETH for gas costs.
You can also play on mobile! If you have the Trust wallet, or Toshi/ Coinbase wallet, you can play in their dApp browser! Yes, Goat Clash from your phone. The way it works is, when you place your bet it takes roughly 1 minute for it to settle that bet. Why? Well, because the randomness and winner is decided based on the TX hash from the block, so it's all written into the smart contract. As soon as that is settled, you will either win, or have to try again!
